From The Archives

arkibo (ar/ki/bo) derives from the Philippine language tagalog meaning archives. This choice of name signifies a profound connection to the concept of preserving, cherishing, and sharing historical elements.

Within our shop, the underlying mission is twofold: sustainability and accessibility. We aim to curate a collection of vintage or historically inspired clothing pieces. These items not only embody a unique sense of style but also hold stories of their own, evoking nostalgia and authenticity.

We aim to offer affordable vintage fashion, connecting eras and cultures sustainably. Each piece isn't just clothing but a bridge between history and present, inviting exploration of our rich past through fashion.
